MANIFESTO

Sugo is a London cycling collective.
But more than that, it’s a group of friends who ride together.

That’s how it started. That’s how it continues.

Sugo was born from the need for a different flavour. What was out there didn’t quite fit. The local cycling scene wasn’t giving us what we were looking for - so we built our own.
Sugo began as a form of expression. Different backgrounds.
Different personalities. Different ways of riding. But under one umbrella.

The best part of Sugo has always been its genuineness and simplicity. No rules. Freestyle. Cani sciolti.

That’s how we described it in the beginning. Raw. Grassroots. A little ignorant in the best way - not chasing metrics, not following formulas. Having fun is the only real parameter.

But we do have a DNA. And a small set of principles. Those won’t change.

We can’t make everyone happy (and we’re not trying to). That’s what keeps this alive. That’s what makes it special. That’s what keeps it ours.

Sugo is open, and it will stay open. Inclusivity is what makes it special. A mix of cultures, mentalities, and shared passion.

We don’t care if you’re fast or slow. We don’t care about your Strava. We don’t care what bike you ride. The best you can do is more than enough.

There’s always a place here. But if you’re part of it, you help build it.

We don’t ask for much. Just be alive in the group. Show up. Be present. Lend a hand when you can. If you can’t, be grateful. Buy a round sometimes. Honour what this is.

A community is something you can count on — for fun, for support, or just to escape the daily routine. In a big city like London, where there are so many clubs, yet it’s still easy to feel alone.

This is a community that gets along. Doesn’t pretend. Doesn’t complain. Be yourself. No judgement.

We don’t use terms like “ride leader.” It doesn’t match our vibe.
Some riders naturally give more - and that deserves recognition.
But life changes. Today it’s me. Tomorrow it’s you.

Know your level. Ride within it. Match the group’s pace. Wait at the top of climbs. Only choose the harder ride only if you’re ready for it.
But we improve, together.

We don’t set limits on ambition. Being part of Sugo means sharing ideas, projects, and goals that might not always be clearly defined - but they’re felt.

No egos. No pretending. Just bikes, people, and whatever the road ahead throws us.

For the love of carbs.
